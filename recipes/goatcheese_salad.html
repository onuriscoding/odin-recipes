Description

Today, you will learn how to make a great goat cheese salad.

Ingredients

  • Salad
  • Goat cheese
  • Strawberries
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Put the salad
  2. Put some olive oil and vinegar
  3. Put the goat cheese in the oven for 3 minutes
  4. Add the goat cheese and some strawberries in your salad
