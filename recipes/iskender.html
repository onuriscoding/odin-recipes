Recipe of Iskender

Description

Iskender is a famous turkish meal.

It is one of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Yoghurt
  • Green pepper
  • Tomato sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Grill the bread
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Put the bread and the meat on top
  4. Put the tomato sauce
  5. Serve it with yoghurt and grilled green pepper
  6. To make it better, you can put liquid butter
