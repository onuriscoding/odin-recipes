Protein Pancakes Recipe

Description

Today, you are going to learn how to make delicious protein pancakes.

We all love pancakes and would love to eat more. However, "normal"pancakes are often not that healthy...

You won't hold yourself anymore because these pancakes are delicious and healthy at the same time!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 500 grams Oath
  • 1 glass of milk/coconut milk
  • Vanilla syrup/sugar
  • 140 grams greek yoghurt

Steps

  • Put all of the ingredients in a mixer and mix
  • Put a little bit of olive oil and cook your pancakes
  • Serve it with some fruits and maple syrup!
